Detailed Description

Definition of a traditional (pre-PBR) pass.

Author
Sylvain DOREMUS
Version
0.1
Date
09/02/2010
Remarks
A traditional pass is composed of : base colours (ambient, diffuse, specular, emissive), and shininess.
